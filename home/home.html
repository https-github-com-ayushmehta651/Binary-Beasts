About Us

"Hunger is not a problem. It is an obscenity. How wonderful it is that
nobody need wait a single moment before starting to improve the world.
- Anne Frank "

Hunger is more than missing a meal. We at "Food Donation" contribute food to the one's who need them. To bring social change in every individual in order to reduce food waste and to make the World Hunger Free. We know it is important for you that your donation is being used effectively and it is donated to the Organization that you wish to. It is very important for us too that all the donations are sent to right people. India is a global agricultural powerhouse, but there are a lot of people who die due to hunger. There is lot of food wasted on a daily basis. We can reduce the wastage of that food and give to the people who don't eat even a meal in a day. Every Contribution you make is very valuable for every individual.
